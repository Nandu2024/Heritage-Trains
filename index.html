Heritage Trains

The Orient Express

The Orient Express is the world’s most legendary luxury train.
It first ran in 1883, connecting Paris to Istanbul.
Known for its opulent carriages and famous passengers,
it inspired countless stories and remains a symbol of romance and adventure.

The Blue Train is South Africa’s premier luxury rail journey.
It travels between Pretoria and Cape Town in grand style.
Guests enjoy gourmet cuisine, fine wines, and stunning scenery.
The train is renowned for its royal blue carriages and five-star service.

The Blue Train

Palace on Wheels

Palace on Wheels is India’s most famous heritage train.
It offers a royal journey through Rajasthan’s historic cities.
The train features luxurious cabins and traditional hospitality.
Experience the grandeur of India’s royal past on this unique ride.

The Rocky Mountaineer travels through Canada’s breathtaking Rockies.
Panoramic windows offer spectacular mountain and forest views.
The journey is known for its comfort, cuisine, and storytelling.
It’s a must for anyone seeking scenic rail adventure in North America.

The Rocky Mountaineer

The Ghan

The Ghan is Australia’s iconic transcontinental train.
It crosses the Outback from Darwin to Adelaide.
Passengers enjoy luxury, comfort, and unique off-train excursions.
The Ghan is a legendary way to experience Australia’s vast interior.

The Jacobite Steam Train runs through the Scottish Highlands.
Its route includes the famous Glenfinnan Viaduct from Harry Potter.
The vintage steam engine and carriages evoke a bygone era.
It’s one of the world’s most scenic and nostalgic train journeys.
